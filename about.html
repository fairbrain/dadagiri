About Dutta’s

Founded in 2020, Dutta’s is a proud Indian brand dedicated to delivering the authentic taste of traditional snacks with uncompromising quality and consistency. Our mission is to blend heritage recipes with modern food-safety standards, ensuring every product embodies both authenticity and trust.

At Dutta’s, we believe that great taste begins with great ingredients. Every batch is crafted using premium-grade raw materials, advanced processing technology, and strict quality checks at every stage of production. From our crunchy chanachur and crispy jhuri bhaja to our classic mung dal, each snack reflects the perfect balance of traditional flavour and modern hygiene.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility operates under stringent FSSAI-approved protocols and adheres to international food-safety norms, en suring freshness and shelf stability without the use of artificial preservatives or additives.

With a growing distribution network and a focus on customer satisfaction, Dutta’s has emerged as a trusted name in the Indian snacks industry. Our core values—quality, integrity, and innovation—drive us to continuously enhance our product range and packaging to meet evolving market demands.

As we look to the future, Dutta’s remains committed to preserving India’s snacking heritage, while bringing modern efficiency, responsible sourcing, and world-class standards to every product we make.
